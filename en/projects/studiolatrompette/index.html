Client

Studio Latrompette

This multidisciplinary studio is a creative agency with 15 years of experience in digital content creation. Its expertise extends far beyond production, encompassing design, direction, and digital polishing. Its greatest strength: cinematography. Here, the request was to create a logo inspired by a camera frame without resorting to its usual archetype.


The resulting logo is derived from the letters L and T, which refer to the studio's name. The logo is clear, clean, and symbolic of its name and the services it offers.

Original and creative concepts are essential for standing out. The website features a playful physical representation of the logo. This attracts attention and enhances the visual experience, while also referencing the studio's services.
